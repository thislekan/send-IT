How it works

We receive your package from the pickup location you provide, and set out to deliver the package to the point of delivery.

We set out to deliver your package, while giving you live updates of your package's location. We monitor if your package destination changes, so we can change course of delivery.

We deliver your package safely, ensuring you get a feedback from your contact, while we await feedback from you.
